About Us

Spotless Cleaning Services was established in 2005 with just one woman, as time went on she started to crceate a name of for herself by her exceptional work. This woman is Carmen Mejia, a migrant of Honduras who resided in Dallas Texas in 1994. As years went on her work became more and more creating a weeks full of work with no help. As she is also a wife and mother to four kids, after long days she continued to work at home for her family. It was in 2008 when she could no longer to do her job without help, so she created her own business that became very successful.
